ZenPlayer Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-05 | Last Updated: 2026-03-05

ZenPlayer is provided by JiongXing Liang. We value your privacy and explain below how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Process

  • The app does not provide account registration or login and does not actively collect identity information such as your name, phone number, or ID number.
  • To provide browsing and playback features, the app sends network requests to content APIs and media resource URLs.
  • When you actively use the download feature, downloaded files and download status are stored locally on your device for offline playback and resume support.
  • Player preferences (for example, denoise level) may be stored locally on your device to improve user experience.

2. Permissions and Purposes

  • Network access: used to load categories, lists, playback URLs, and media files.
  • Background audio: used to continue audio playback when the app is in the background.
  • Local file read/write and file sharing: used to save files you choose to download and support export/sharing actions you initiate.

3. Third-Party Components

The app may use essential third-party open-source components (for example, image caching) for core functionality. We do not integrate ad SDKs, do not run personalized ads, and do not sell personal information.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Downloaded content and related records are primarily stored on your device.
  • You can remove related data by deleting downloaded files or uninstalling the app.
  • We take reasonable security measures, but no network transmission can be guaranteed to be absolutely secure.

5. Children

Minors should use the app under the guidance of a parent or legal guardian.
